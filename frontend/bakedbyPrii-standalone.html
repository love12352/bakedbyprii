Our little story

Born in a home kitchen, baked for your celebrations.

bakedbyPrii started the way most good things do — with a craving and a mixing bowl. What began as weekend baking for family quickly turned into a tiny home bakery, where every cookie, cake and cupcake is made to order, never mass-produced.

No preservatives, no shortcuts. Just real butter, good chocolate, and a whole lot of love poured into small batches.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · bakedbyPrii, Didcot, Oxfordshire, UK

Who we are

bakedbyPrii is a home bakery based in Didcot, UK. When you place an order or contact us, we collect and use your personal data to fulfil that order. We are the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR.

What data we collect

  • Name, phone number, and email address — to confirm and deliver your order
  • Order details (item, date, special requests) — to bake exactly what you need
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we never see your card number

How we use your data

  • To process and fulfil your order
  • To send you an order confirmation and updates
  • To contact you if there is a problem with your order
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing

How long we keep it

Order records are kept for 2 years for accounting purposes, then securely deleted. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@bakedbyprii.com.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@bakedbyprii.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).
