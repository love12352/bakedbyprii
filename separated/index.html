Our little story

Born in a home kitchen, baked for your celebrations.

Sugar & Crumb started the way most good things do — with a craving and a mixing bowl. What began as weekend baking for family quickly turned into a tiny home bakery, where every cookie, cake and cupcake is made to order, never mass-produced.

No preservatives, no shortcuts. Just real butter, good chocolate, and a whole lot of love poured into small batches.
